Description

These Alchohol Trays are hand crafted from the finest wood species. CNC programmed and hand finished for quality. There are several options to choose from. They're made in two different 15 in diameter 1.25 in thick unique models. The Basic model is stained pine equipped with 4 feet, six custom pockets and a array of Engraved custom tribal patterns. The premium custom Version takes exotic to another level. This version has 4 handles built to carry up to 35lbs and a extra lower foot that allows for rotating with the flick of a finger. These works of art are for any and all occasions. The starting price is $80 dollars.
